Innovative sequential delivery of NSAID and PPIŁ

The first arthritis therapy to provide sequential delivery of an NSAID and a PPI in a single tablet. IR esomeprazole is released in the stomach prior to the dissolution of naproxen in the small intestine.1
IR esomeprazole (20mg) helps to improve upper-GI tolerance1,3

Dosing and Administration:

• The dose for VIMOVO is 1 tablet (500mg/20mg) twice daily.
• VIMOVO must be swallowed whole with water, and not split, chewed, or crushed. It is recommended that VIMOVO be taken at least 30 minutes prior to food intake.
• Undesirable effects of naproxen may be minimized by using the lowest effective dose for the shortest duration possible.
• In patients not treated with an NSAID previously, a lower daily dose of naproxen or another NSAID should be considered.
• When total daily dose of 1000mg of naproxen is not considered appropriate, alternative therapeutic regimens should be utilized.
