Study Design:

A network meta-analysis of cardiovascular safety of NSAIDs performed by Integrating all available direct and indirect evidence, including all large-scale, randomised trials comparing any NSAID with other NSAIDs or placebo ( 31 trials in 116, 429 patients with more than 115,000 patient years of follow-up were included). Patients were allocated to naproxen, ibuprofen, diclofenac, celecoxib, etoricoxib, rofecoxib, lumiracoxib or placebo.1
