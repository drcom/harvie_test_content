Study Design:

Study searches included Medline, Embase an Cochrane central trials register (studies included in the total statistical analysis of a mixed treatment comparison). Comparisons based on inclusion of data from all licensed doses (Dose ranges: Naproxen 500-1000mg/day, Ibuprofen 30-800mg tid or qid/day, Diclofenac 100-150mg/day, Celecoxib 200mg/day, Etoricoxib 60mg/day). Differences in trial duration (4-12 weeks) were independently assessed to minimize bias. All NSAIDs analysed were significantly better than placebo. ES- effect size of indirect comparison. Crl-credibleinterval. MTC – mixed treatment comparison.
