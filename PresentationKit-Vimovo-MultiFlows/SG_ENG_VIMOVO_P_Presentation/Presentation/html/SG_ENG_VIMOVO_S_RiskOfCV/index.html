Study Design:

A systematic review of community-based controlled observational studies that involved comprehensive literature searches, extracting adjusted relative risk (RR) estimates, and pooling the estimates for major CV events associated with use of individual NSAIDs, in different doses, and in populations with low and high background risks of CV events, Thirty case-control studies included 184,946 CV events, and 21 cohort studies described outcomes in > 2.7 million exposed individuals.
