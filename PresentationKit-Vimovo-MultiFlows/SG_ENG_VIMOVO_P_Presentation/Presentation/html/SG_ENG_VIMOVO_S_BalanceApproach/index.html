* Balance is provided by a fixed-dose combination of enteric-coated (EC) naproxen for pain relief and immediate-release esomeprazole for upper-GI tolerability. Helping to improve upper-GI tolerability is evidenced by fewer upper-GI ulcers, fewer discontinuations due to upper-GI AEs including duodenal ulcers, and less abdominal discomfort over a 6-month period as measured by dyspepsia symptoms versus EC naproxen.
