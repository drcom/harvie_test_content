Pivotal Trials:

Having a medical condition expected to require daily NSAID therapy for at least 6 months. Patients with clinician-diagnosed osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis or any other condition. 50 years or older or 18-49 with a documented history of gastric or duodenal ulcer within past 5 years. 83% of patients were 50-69 years of age. The prospectively defined primary efficacy endpoint was the cumulative incidence of gastric ulcer
(> 3mm diameter with depth) observed by endoscopy at 1, 3 and 6 months.1,2
