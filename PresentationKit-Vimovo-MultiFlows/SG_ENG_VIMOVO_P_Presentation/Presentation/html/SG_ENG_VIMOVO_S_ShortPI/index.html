Please consult local full prescribing information before prescribing. VIMOVO™ (500mg naproxen & 20mg esomeprazole tablet). INDICATIONS: Relief of signs and symptoms of osteoarthritis, rheumatoid arthritis, and ankylosing spondylitis and to decrease the risk of developing gastric ulcers in patients at risk of developing NSAID associated gastric ulcers. Not recommended for initial treatment of acute pain. DOSAGE: 1 tablet twice daily. CONTRAINDICATIONS: Hypersensitivity to any component of this product. History of asthma, urticaria or allergic-type reactions induced by
administration of aspirin or other NSAIDs, third trimester of pregnancy, severe hepatic impairment, during peri-operative period in the setting of CABG
surgery, severe heart failure, severe renal impairment, active peptic ulceration, gastrointestinal bleeding, cerebrovascular bleeding or other
bleeding disorders. PRECAUTIONS: GI bleeding, ulceration or perforation has been reported with all NSAIDs. VIMOVO has been formulated with esomeprazole to decrease the incidence of GI side effects. Caution in the elderly and in patients with uncontrolled hypertension, congestive heart failure, established ischaemic heart disease, peripheral arterial disease and/or cerebrovascular disease, impaired renal function, coagulation disorders or receiving drug therapy that interferes with haemostasis, at high risk of bleeding and on full anti-coagulation therapy, history of angio-oedema, bronchospastic reactivity, rhinitis and nasal polyps, or pre-existing asthma. Avoid use with COX-2 selective inhibitors. UNDESIRABLE EFFECTS: No new safety findings were identified during VIMOVO treatment compared to the well-established safety profiles of the individual active substances. Common undesirable effects: Dyspepsia, dizziness, headache, taste disturbance, hypertension, abdominal pain, constipation, diarrhea, esophagitis, flatulence, gastric/duodenal ulcers (as detected by scheduled routine endoscopy), gastritis, nausea, vomiting, skin rashes, arthralgia, oedema. 10/AA/SG/PAIN.000-175-774.3.0.
